## 🌍 Overview

**EcoSphere** is an interactive Carbon Footprint Awareness Platform designed to help individuals understand, calculate, and reduce their environmental impact.

The platform helps users estimate their carbon emissions from daily activities, analyze emission sources, and receive personalized suggestions for adopting sustainable habits.

Built for:

**PromptWars Virtual 2026 | Hack2skill Challenge 3**

# 🎯 Problem Statement

Climate change is increasing due to carbon emissions from transportation, electricity consumption, food choices, and lifestyle activities.

Many people are unaware of their personal carbon footprint and the actions they can take to reduce environmental impact.

EcoSphere helps users:

- Calculate their carbon footprint
- Understand emission sources
- Get personalized sustainability recommendations
- Build eco-friendly habits

---

# ✨ Features

## 🧮 Carbon Footprint Calculator

Users can calculate emissions from:

### 🚗 Transportation

- Vehicle fuel type
- Travel distance
- Public transport usage
- Flight emissions

### ⚡ Home Energy

- Electricity consumption
- Regional grid emission factors
- Renewable energy impact

### 🍽️ Food Lifestyle

- Diet preferences
- Food waste impact

### ♻️ Waste Management

- Shopping habits
- Recycling activities

---

## 📊 Carbon Analysis Dashboard

Provides:

- Total CO₂ emission estimate
- Category-wise emission breakdown
- Impact analysis
- Sustainability insights

---

## 🤖 Sustainability Advisor

Provides personalized suggestions:

- Reduce electricity consumption
- Choose greener transportation
- Improve daily habits
- Lower carbon emissions

---

## 🏆 Eco Pledges & Green Actions

Users can:

- Take environmental pledges
- Earn impact points
- Track sustainable actions
- Reduce their estimated footprint
